About Habify

Habify is a web-based application that was created to help you acheive the goals that will you have set to become a better version of yourself. At Habify, we understand that it is easy to set goals, but it is difficult to remain committed to those goals that you have set. That is why we have created an online tool that will keep track of your habits so you know what to keep working on to become the dream version of yourself.

Together with our 'Personal' plan, we also offer a 'Couple's' plan as well as a 'Family' plan so that you can build a good habit together with your loved ones or work on separate goals all at the same time.
